Virtual Mouse

What is Virtual Mouse?

Virtual Mouse is a software application that allows you to control your computer using hand gestures. It is an alternative to a traditional mouse and can be used on any surface, without the need for a physical mouse or touchpad.

How does it work?

Virtual Mouse uses computer vision technology to track the movements of your hand and translate them into mouse movements on the screen. It can detect different hand gestures such as clicks, scrolls, and drags, allowing you to control your computer just like you would with a physical mouse.

Advantages of Virtual Mouse

  • No need for a physical mouse or touchpad
  • Can be used on any surface
  • Allows for more natural and intuitive control
  • Can reduce the risk of repetitive strain injuries
