About Online Construction

Online Construction is a web-based platform that allows customers to plan and manage construction projects from start to finish. Our platform provides a range of services, including project planning, cost estimation, contractor management, and progress tracking, all in one place.

Features:

  • Online Document Verification
  • 3D Design Model of Building
  • Interior and Style Selection
  • Product Choosing
  • Estmiation of Captial and Time
  • Progress Tracking and Daily Reports

Our Goals

  • To Save Time for Client
  • Better Communication and Interaction between Client and Company
  • Daily Updates and Reports will be Updated
  • To Save the Cost of Capital for Client
  • Reduction in Time for Construction
